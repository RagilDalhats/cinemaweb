The Boy

Film Type : Thriller

Producer : Matt Berenson, Jodyne Herron, Gary Lucchesi, Tom Rosenberg, Jim Wedaa

Director : William Brent Bell

Writer : Stacey Menear

Production : Lakeshore Entertainment, Huayi Brothers Pictures, Vertigo Entertainment, STXfilms

Synopsis

Greta accepts the job of a nanny for a wealthy couple's child and is disturbed when she finds out the child is a doll. However, when she mishandles the doll, she is shocked to find that it is alive.
